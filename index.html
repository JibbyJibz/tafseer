Why is Tafaseer Important?

  • The Quran was not revealed only to be read, but rather it must be pondered upon.
  • To have knowledge of why and when the Ayah was revealed

Some of the most important things you will learn are

  1. Why an Ayah was Revealed
  2. When an Ayah was Revealed
  3. How should the Ayah be applie in our life
